Rules

Guess the KORDLE in 6 tries.
Each guess must be a valid 5 letter word. Press enter to submit.
After each guess, the color of the *letter will change to show how close your guess was to the word.


Examples

 S   H   R   E   K 
The letter S is in the word and in the correct spot.

 L   O   V   E   S 
The letter O is in the word but in the wrong spot.

 F   I   O   N   A 
The letter N is not in the word in any spot.
